Our story

Rooted in tradition. Elevated for modern skin.

Skin-Bone was born from a simple belief: the most effective skincare is often the most elemental. We partner with regenerative ranches to source grassfed tallow, then refine it with botanicals worthy of a modern ritual.

From heritage to atelier

The Skin-Bone method

Each formula is developed to calm, protect, and nourish.

Slow rendering

We gently render tallow at low temperatures to preserve vitamins and fatty acids.

Botanical infusion

We steep botanicals for 72 hours, creating a concentrated, aromatic infusion.

Air-whipped texture

We whip the finished blend to create a soft balm that melts on contact.
