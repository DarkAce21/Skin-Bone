FAQ

Your questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about tallow skincare and Skin-Bone rituals.

Is tallow safe for acne-prone skin?

Our tallow blends are non-comedogenic and balance sebum production. We recommend the Renewal Cream for acne-prone skin and a patch test for sensitive users.

How should I store my tallow cream?

Keep your jar away from direct heat or sunlight. The texture may soften in warm climates.

Are your products vegan?

No. Skin-Bone is centered on grassfed tallow. We choose ethically sourced animal ingredients.

Do you offer refills?

Yes. Select sizes offer refill pods. Join our newsletter to be notified of refill drops.

How long does a jar last?

Our 50 ml jars last 6-8 weeks with daily use; 100 ml jars last 3-4 months.
